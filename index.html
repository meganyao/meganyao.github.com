About me

Yuxia is currently working in HP Labs Singapore. Her main interests include cloud computing, focusing on big data storage, social media analytics and more.

In her spare time, she exercises regularly and practices Tai Chi every day. She is also invovled in volunteering service at MINDS MYG.
